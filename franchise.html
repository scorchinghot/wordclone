FAQ

1. What are the terms of the Franchise Agreement?
10-year term, $50K fee, 4.5% royalty on gross sales.
2. What does the franchise fee include?
It includes use of brand, initial training, marketing materials, and setup support.
3. Do I need restaurant experience?
While it's preferred, it's not required. We provide full training to get you up to speed.
4. How long does it take to open?
Typically, it takes 3–6 months from signing to grand opening depending on location readiness.
